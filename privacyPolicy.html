1. Introduction

Welcome to Hirah Attar. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or interact with our services.

By using our website, contacting us, or placing an order, you agree to the practices described in this policy. We handle your information responsibly and transparently, with the same care and integrity that define our fragrances and customer relationships.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Full name used for orders and communication
  • Delivery and billing address details
  • Email address for order updates and support
  • Phone number used for delivery coordination

Technical & Usage Data

  • IP address and approximate location (for security purposes)
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Website interaction data (pages visited, time spent)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

6. Cookies & Tracking Technology

To provide a refined and seamless browsing experience, Hirah Attar uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. These tools help us remember your preferences, improve site performance, and ensure a smooth purchasing journey.

Essential

Required for core functionality such as cart operations, secure checkout, and order processing.

Functional

Stores preferences like language, region, and recently viewed products to enhance usability.

Analytical

Helps us analyze traffic and interaction patterns so we can improve performance and content relevance.

You may control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that restricting essential cookies may affect site functionality and order completion.

7. Your Legal Rights & Data Portability

Hirah Attar respects your full control over your personal information. We operate in alignment with global data protection standards, including GDPR (EU/UK) and CCPA, regardless of your geographic location.

Right to Access & Rectification

You may request a detailed summary of the personal data we hold about you and ask for corrections if any information is inaccurate or incomplete.

Right to Erasure (Right to Be Forgotten)

You may request deletion of your personal data from our systems, except where retention is required for legal, accounting, or security obligations.

Data Portability & Processing Objections

You may obtain your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format or object to specific processing activities such as promotional communications.
